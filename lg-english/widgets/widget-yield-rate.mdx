The `yield rate` is equal to the average between the `maximum margin` and the `minimum margin` with :

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="maximum margin" icon="gauge-max">
**\+** `Price offered for sale` of items currently for sale **\-** `Purchase price` of items for sale<br />
**\+** `Advised selling price` of items currently in stock **\-** `Purchase price` of items currently in stock<br />
**\+** `Selling price` of items sold **\-** `Purchase price` of items sold<br />
**\+** Bulk `selling price` **\-** Bulk `purchase price`<br />
**\-** `Fees`
### Divided by
**\+** `Purchase price` of items for sale<br />
**\+** `Purchase price` of items currently in stock<br />
**\+** `Purchase price` of items sold<br />
**\+** Bulk `purchase price`
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="minimum margin" icon="gauge-min">
**\+** `Advised selling price` of items currently for sale **\-** `Purchase price` of items for sale<br />
**\+** `Advised second-hand selling price` of items currently in stock **\-** `Purchase price` of items currently in stock<br />
**\+** `Selling price` of items sold **\-** `Purchase price` of items sold<br />
**\+** Bulk `selling price` **\-** Bulk `purchase price`<br />
**\-** `Fees`
### Divided by
**\+** `Purchase price` of items for sale<br />
**\+** `Purchase price` of items currently in stock<br />
**\+** `Purchase price` of items sold<br />
**\+** Bulk `purchase price`<br />
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

The `annual yield rate` is equal to the `yield rate` / number of months since the `investment start date` ([settings](https://www.legaussitot.com/settings-user.php)) * 12
